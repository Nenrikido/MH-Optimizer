MH Wilds Build Optimizer (Beta)

from @nenrikido on discord
How to use :
  • Type your desired skills and sets in the input boxes (autocomplete available)
  • Choose your max amount of skill points and the weight of each skill, and the min amount of pieces from each set
  • Click on "Optimize" and wait for the results
Notes :
  • For now, builds are only calculated using the dimensius weapon (artian with 3-3-3 decoration slots)
  • Data is up to date with TU2 (AT Uth Duna)
  • Yes the default skills and sets chosen are for LS, yes i'm a weeb
